# Product Overview

Lazeez VORP (Vendor Operations & Resource Platform) is an enterprise-grade ERP system for managing vendor relationships, operations, and workforce intelligence.

## Core Domains

### Vendor Management
- Vendor onboarding, profiles, and lifecycle tracking
- SAFI Score system (vendor performance scoring)
- Vendor-specific remarks, payments, and assignments
- MOU (Memorandum of Understanding) creation, tracking, and renewal
- MOU Vault with AI-powered document extraction (OCR + AI analysis)
- Vendor payment tracking (upfront, remaining, commission-based)

### Issue Tracking & Project Management
- Multi-status issue workflow (open, in_progress, resolved, closed)
- Priority-based issue management (low, medium, high, critical)
- Vendor-linked issue tracking
- Project board with task management
- AI-powered issue analysis and resolution suggestions

### HR & Performance Module
- Organization hierarchy (Business Units → Departments → Teams → Employees)
- 6-vector performance scoring system (Output, Efficiency, Quality, Reliability, Behavioral, Contextual)
- Employee lifecycle management (hire, transfer, promote, offboard)
- Attendance tracking and leave management
- 360° appraisal system (peer, manager, self, subordinate reviews)
- Time tracking and resource planning
- Employee-vendor assignment management

### Finance Module
- Revenue management and commission tracking
- Vendor payout calculation and scheduling
- Subscription billing management
- Accounts receivable and payable
- Expense management with approval workflows
- Financial modeling workspace (Excel-like sheets)
- P&L automation and cash flow tracking
- AI-powered financial forecasting
- Receipt vault with categorization (riders, vendors, general)

### Analytics & Reporting
- Real-time dashboards with KPI tracking
- Data export capabilities
- Audit log system with comprehensive change tracking
- Financial intelligence and projections
- Performance analytics and trend visualization

## User Roles & Permissions
- Admin: Full system access, user approvals, custom role management
- HR/Staff: Workforce management, performance tracking, department oversight
- Manager: Department-level access, team management, approvals
- Employee: Self-service access, task management, feedback submission
- Vendor: Limited portal access (future consideration)

## Key Features
- Role-based access control (RBAC) with custom permissions
- Real-time notifications and activity feeds
- Calendar integration (MOUs, issues, payments, leave, renewals)
- AI-powered features (document extraction, issue analysis, financial forecasting)
- Audit trail for all critical operations
- Multi-currency support (planned)
- Workflow automation (vendor-specific, approval chains)
